HOLIDAY!

Let’s face it with the holidays coming up everyone is ready to celebrate in a big way, maybe even get out of the dingy sweats, possibly even get dolled up (that may just be me in dingy sweats), and spend time with those we care about- Finally! No more social distancing, from family, friends, and best of all no more facetime, just real-life interactions and togetherness this holiday.


We should not forget that during the dark days of COVID-19 2020 Florist’s provided us with a creative way to let our loved ones know we miss them. Sending flowers is one of them. It certainly didn’t have to be a special occasion or holiday; it was just another way of saying, “I’m thinking of you!” and I miss and care about you. Flowers mean so much. The gesture of giving them, or sending them, is a sign of love, friendship, respect, and admiration. And they make everyone smile.


With so many holiday gift options available now as the country is reopened please do not forget about the one that had your back during the gloomiest of days -your Local Florist, who was open for business as hard as it was and there for us when we could not be there in person for our family and friends.
